What Is Petto

Petto is a group of products aimed to increase family bonding time, for happier families.

In every bundle that you buy, you will get a home station and 2 watches. Additional watches can be bought for the rest of your loved ones separately.

The Watch

Our watch for Petto has an amazing feature in the form of the pet that you can take care of. Your watch and pet are also used in the games on the home station.

The watch is like every other smartwatch but it makes sure to take into account your feelings.

The Home Station

Everyone knows what Google Home is. For Petto, we have the home station, the Petto Home.

The Petto Home is a centralised schedular and a game station for your family. You would be able to play games and schedule family time. It would also be like the announcement center for your family's feelings.
