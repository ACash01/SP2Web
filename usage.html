Using The Watch

The watch is a traditional smartwatch so swipe functions are to be expected.

Additional functions are accessed by the buttons or even voice commands. The buttons will be for controller-like use for minigames playable on the home station.

Using The Watch

Petto Home can be placed anywhere in your house, but the best spot is in your living room.

Using it is not to be a hassle, mainly since it has telegraphed actions that you can take. Watches would also instantly be synchronised to the Petto Home when you put them to charge.
